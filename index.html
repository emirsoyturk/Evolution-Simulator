What is Evolution

There are four principles at work in evolution, variation, inheritance, selection, time

Variation

Variation is the process by which new genetic material is introduced into a population.

Inheritance

Inheritance is the process by which genetic material is passed from one generation to the next.

Selection

Selection is the process by which individuals that have inherited favourable traits produce more offspring than individuals that have not.

Time

Time is the process by which the process of evolution occurs.
